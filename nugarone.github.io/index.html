Common Questions

Will it disrupt my staff or take up their time?

No. We handle delivery, install, stocking, and repairs ourselves. Your team doesn't need to do anything — refills and maintenance happen without any input from your staff.

What if it doesn't sell?

There's no cost or commitment either way — you only earn when it sells, and you're never charged for machine time that doesn't convert. If a venue isn't the right fit, we'll say so upfront.

Who owns the stock and machine?

Nugarone owns and stocks the machine. You're simply hosting it and earning a share of the revenue it generates — no purchase, no inventory risk on your side.

Can we cancel?

Yes. There's no long-term contract — if it's not working for your venue, we'll remove the machine at no cost to you.
